Our Products

Georgettes

Georgettes are lightweight and flowy fabrics with a slightly crinkled texture. They are often used in creating elegant and draping garments, adding a touch of sophistication and grace to any outfit.

Chinons

Chinons are a type of lightweight and sheer fabric, known for their soft drape and delicate appearance. Often used in creating ethereal and romantic outfits, Chinons offer a graceful and feminine aesthetic.

Chiffons

Chiffons are sheer, lightweight fabrics with a subtle shimmer and a soft drape. Renowned for their ethereal and flowing nature, chiffons add an elegant and delicate touch to dresses, blouses, and scarves.

Viscos

Viscos is a versatile fabric known for its soft and smooth texture. It offers excellent draping qualities and is often used in creating comfortable and breathable clothing items, making it perfect for both casual and formal wear.

Nylons

Nylon is a durable and resilient synthetic fabric widely used for its strength and elasticity. It is known for its smooth texture and is commonly used in activewear, hosiery, and outerwear for its moisture-wicking and quick-drying properties.

Organzas

Organza is a sheer and crisp fabric with a slight sheen. Its lightweight and stiff nature make it ideal for creating elegant overlays, ruffles, and decorative accents, adding a touch of glamour to dresses and formal wear.
